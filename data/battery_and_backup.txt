Battery and Backup System – EnergInAI

1. Battery Sizing Formula
Battery (Ah) = Load (W) × Backup Hours ÷ System Voltage

2. Backup Requirements
- Normal home → 3 hours
- Heavy home → 4–6 hours
- Office → 2 hours

3. Battery Types
- Lithium → long life, fast charge
- Lead Acid → budget option

4. Issues:
- Backup kam → battery health low
- Overload → inverter trip
- Slow charging → wiring issue
- Battery smell → needs replacement
