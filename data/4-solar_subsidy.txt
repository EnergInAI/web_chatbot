INTENT: solar_subsidy

Examples:
- How much subsidy will I get for solar?
- Government solar subsidy in 2025?
- Tell me about MNRE subsidy

Short Response:
Subsidy: ₹30k (1 kW), ₹60k (2 kW), ₹78k (3 kW+).

Long Response:
As per MNRE 2025 (PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana), subsidy is ₹30,000 for 1 kW, ₹60,000 for 2 kW, and ₹78,000 for 3 kW or above, subject to DISCOM verification and approval.
