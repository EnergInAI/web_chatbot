EnergInAI Device Troubleshooting

1. Device not connecting:
- Check WiFi password
- Router distance < 8 meters
- Restart device

2. No data?
- Device inverter output par ho sakta hai
- MCB ON hona chahiye

3. Voltage/Current wrong?
- Loose wiring
- CT sensor wrong placement
- Technician needed

4. Spikes in graph?
- AC compressor start
- Motor loads
- Bad wiring joint
